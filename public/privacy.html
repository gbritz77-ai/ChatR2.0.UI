Privacy Policy

Last updated: 9 April 2026  ·  Outsec (Pty) Ltd

ChatR ("the App") is a private team communication platform operated by Outsec (Pty) Ltd ("we", "us", "our"). This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Who this app is for

ChatR is intended exclusively for authorised employees and contractors of organisations that have been granted access by Outsec. It is not available to the general public and is not directed at children under 13.

2. Data we collect

  • Account information – username and email address, provided by your organisation's administrator.
  • Messages – text messages, emoji reactions, and group membership information you create within the App.
  • Profile avatar – an optional photo you choose to upload.
  • Availability schedule – optional working hours you set in your profile.
  • Usage data – connection timestamps and last-seen time, used to show online/offline status.
  • Device permissions – camera and microphone access, requested only when you initiate or join a video call. We do not record calls.

3. Data we do not collect

  • We do not collect advertising identifiers or track you across other apps or websites.
  • We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.
  • We do not use analytics SDKs (e.g. Firebase Analytics, Google Analytics).

4. How we use your data

  • To authenticate you and maintain your session.
  • To deliver messages and video call signalling to other users in your organisation.
  • To display your name, avatar, and availability to colleagues.
  • To operate and improve the App's reliability and security.

5. Data storage and security

All data is stored on servers located in South Africa (AWS af-south-1 region). Data is transmitted over HTTPS and authenticated with short-lived JWT tokens. Passwords are stored as bcrypt hashes and are never stored or transmitted in plain text.

6. Data retention

Your account and messages are retained for as long as your organisation's account with Outsec is active. When an account is deactivated, personal data is deleted within 30 days upon request.

7. Your rights

You may request at any time:

  • Access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction of inaccurate data.
  • Deletion of your account and associated data.

Contact your organisation's administrator or email us directly (see Section 9).

8. Third-party services

The App uses the following infrastructure providers, each subject to their own privacy policies:

  • Amazon Web Services (AWS) – cloud hosting, storage, and content delivery.
  • Metered.ca – TURN relay servers used only during active video calls to traverse firewalls. No call content is stored.
